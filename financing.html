Financing FAQ

Answers to common questions about our financing options.

What credit score is needed?

We offer financing options for a wide range of credit profiles. While promotional 0% APR offers typically require good to excellent credit, our low monthly payment plans are available for more credit situations. Apply to see your personalized options.

Is there a penalty for early payoff?

No! There are no prepayment penalties with any of our financing partners. You can pay off your balance at any time without additional fees.

Can I use financing with promotions?

Yes, you can combine special financing offers with most of our current promotions. Ask your project consultant for details on stacking savings.

How long does approval take?

Most applications receive a decision within seconds to minutes. In some cases, additional verification may be required, extending the process to 24-48 hours.

What is the minimum amount to finance?

Minimum financing amounts vary by program, starting as low as $1,000 for our low monthly payment plans and $2,500 for 0% interest promotions.

Do you offer in-house financing?

We partner with top national lenders to offer the best rates and terms. While we don't offer in-house financing, our partners provide competitive options for all customers.
